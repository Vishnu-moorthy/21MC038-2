Graphic Design

Description

This course teaches you the essentials of graphic design, focusing on tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. You will create stunning designs, whether for digital media or print.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic principles of graphic design (color theory, typography, etc.)
  • Using Photoshop and Illustrator for professional design
  • Designing logos, brochures, posters, and web graphics
  • Understanding design concepts for both print and digital media
  • Building a design portfolio
